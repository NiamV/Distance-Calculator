Firstly the what3words addresses are converted into latitude and longitude coordinates. The distance is calculated using spherical trigonometry. The formulae are: $$ cos(\delta) = sin(\phi_1) sin (\phi_2) + cos(\phi_1) cos (\phi_2) cos (L_1 - L_2) $$ $$ d = \delta \times R_{Earth} $$ Where:
  • \( \phi_1 \) is the latitute of place 1
  • \( L_1 \) is the longitude of place 1
  • \( \phi_2 \) is the latitude of place 2
  • \( L_2 \) is the longitude of place 2
  • \( R_{Earth} \) is the radius of the Earth
  • \( d \) is the distance between the two locations
Assuming the angles are converted to radians.
